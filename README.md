# Elder Care Support App

## Project Overview
This project is a mobile application designed to support elderly people and improve emergency medical response.  
The app allows medical personnel to quickly access important health information during emergencies, while also helping elderly users stay socially active by participating in community events.

---

## Objectives
- Provide quick access to elderly medical information in emergency situations
- Reduce medical risks caused by missing or unknown health records
- Encourage elderly people to join social and community activities
- Improve communication between elderly users and healthcare providers

---

## Target Users
- Elderly people
- Medical personnel (doctors, nurses, emergency responders)
- Caregivers and community organizers

---

# Key Features

### For Elderly Users
- View upcoming activities and events
- Register for activities directly through the app
- Manage basic personal information

### For Medical Personnel
- Access emergency medical information
- View allergy records (e.g. drug allergies)
- View medical history and basic health data
- Quickly identify the patient during emergencies
